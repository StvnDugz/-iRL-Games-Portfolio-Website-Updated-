i.R.L. Games

I.R.L. Games is an independent game developer and publisher based in Cork, Ireland. Our company was founded in 2018 by game designer Steven Duggan. Since then, we have created En-Trance , ShinoBoy , i-SO and most notably Wave Runner. Today, we are a team of four creative professionals, coming from a broad range of experience and education. We are hard at work on bringing all our games to more players - and on making new ones.
